Special of the Day

Tofu Delight

Marinated tofu on seasonal greens.

Tofu Delight is a flavorful vegan dish made with golden-fried tofu and fresh seasonal vegetables. It's tossed in a savory soy-based sauce with hints of garlic and ginger. Rich in plant-based protein and full of flavor, this dish is both wholesome and satisfying. Served with your choice of rice or noodles, it's a favorite among plant-based food lovers.

Rainbow Salad

Colorful vegetables with vinaigrette.

Rainbow Salad is a vibrant mix of fresh vegetables and fruits, including carrots, red cabbage, bell peppers, cucumbers, and cherry tomatoes. Each bite delivers a crisp texture and natural sweetness. Lightly tossed in a zesty citrus vinaigrette, this salad is both refreshing and nutritious. It's a perfect choice for a light and healthy meal.

Pumpkin Soup

Warm, creamy and comforting.

Pumpkin Soup is a rich and creamy blend made from fresh pumpkin, garlic, and onion. Its smooth texture and naturally sweet, earthy flavor make it perfect for cooler days. A touch of cream adds depth and richness to every spoonful. Served hot and topped with herbs or a swirl of cream, it's both comforting and satisfying.

Bean Salad

Beans with herbs, lemon dressing.

Bean Salad is a hearty and protein-packed dish made with a colorful mix of kidney beans, chickpeas, and green beans. Fresh herbs and a light lemon dressing add brightness and balance to the rich texture of the beans. Each bite is flavorful, wholesome, and satisfying. It's an ideal choice for a light lunch or a nutritious side.
