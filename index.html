Special of the Day

Tofu Delight

Marinated tofu on seasonal greens.

Tofu Delight is a flavorful vegan dish made with golden-fried tofu and fresh seasonal vegetables. It's tossed in a savory soy-based sauce with hints of garlic and ginger. The dish is both protein-rich and satisfying, perfect for a wholesome meal. Served with rice or noodles, it's a favorite among plant-based food lovers.

Rainbow Salad

Colorful vegetables with citrus vinaigrette.

Rainbow Salad is a colorful and refreshing dish made with a variety of fresh vegetables and fruits. It typically includes ingredients like carrots, red cabbage, bell peppers, cucumbers, and cherry tomatoes. Each bite offers a crisp texture and a burst of natural flavors. Tossed with a light vinaigrette or tangy dressing

Pumpkin Soup

Warm, creamy and comforting.

Pumpkin soup is a creamy and flavorful dish made from fresh pumpkin. It has a smooth texture and a naturally sweet, earthy taste. The soup is often cooked with garlic, onion, and a touch of cream for extra richness. Served hot, it's perfect for cold days. Garnished with herbs or a swirl of cream, it's both comforting and nutritious.
